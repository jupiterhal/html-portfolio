The Best Movies According to Athaliah

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

The Great Gatsby

A story set in the Roaring Twenties filled with wealth, glamour, and hidden despair. It follows Jay Gatsby, a mysterious millionaire obsessed with rekindling lost love. Lavish parties and dazzling lifestyles mask emptiness and longing. Themes of love, ambition, and illusion dominate the narrative. It is a tragic tale of dreams clashing with harsh reality.

The Summer I Turned Pretty ( The Movie)

A coming-of-age drama set during summers at a beach house. It follows Belly, a teenage girl navigating love, friendship, and family. Two brothers complicate her emotions as she discovers herself. The series highlights the beauty and pain of growing up. It blends romance with heartfelt lessons about change.
